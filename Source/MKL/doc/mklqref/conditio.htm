Condition Numbers

?gecon estimates the reciprocal of the condition number of a general matrix in either the 1-norm or the infinity-norm.
call sgecon (norm, n, SA, lda, sanorm, srcond, SWORK, IWORK, info)
call dgecon (norm, n, DA, lda, danorm, drcond, DWORK, IWORK, info)
call cgecon (norm, n, CA, lda, sanorm, srcond, CWORK, SRWORK, info)
call zgecon (norm, n, ZA, lda, danorm, drcond, ZWORK, DRWORK, info)

?gbcon estimates the reciprocal of the condition number of a band matrix in either the 1-norm or the infinity-norm.
call sgbcon (norm, n, kl, ku, SAB, ldab, IPIV, sanorm, srcond, SWORK, IWORK, info)
call dgbcon (norm, n, kl, ku, DAB, ldab, IPIV, danorm, drcond, DWORK, IWORK, info)
call cgbcon (norm, n, kl, ku, CAB, ldab, IPIV, sanorm, srcond, CWORK, SRWORK, info)
call zgbcon (norm, n, kl, ku, ZAB, ldab, IPIV, danorm, drcond, ZWORK, DRWORK, info)
